HCI Researcher

I am John Joon Young Chung, a second-year Ph.D. student at University of Michigan, working with Eytan Adar.

I am interested in designing, building, and studying computational tools for collaborative artistic creations. As the vehicle for the research, I enjoy drawing ideas from various fields, including AI/ML, crowdsourcing, and psychology.
